# Final Active-Version Hierarchy

- Analysis dataset: `analysis_dataset_v1_1`.
- Mortality: `primary_mortality_v1_1`.
- Readmission cause-specific Cox and time-varying HRs: validated `02_readmission_outcomes`.
- Readmission standardized CIF, RD, RR, and additive interaction: `02_readmission_outcomes_v1_2`.
- Sensitivity analyses and integrated summary: `03_sensitivity_analyses` and `04_integrated_results`.

Deprecated readmission CIF v1 and v1.1 outputs are audit history only and must not be used for clinical interpretation.
